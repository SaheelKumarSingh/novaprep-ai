Built by IIM Alumni and AI Pioneers.

Our mission is to democratize access to elite MBA preparation. NovaPrep was founded by a team of alumni from IIM Ahmedabad, Bangalore, Calcutta, and XLRI, in collaboration with deep-learning researchers.

The Problem

Traditional MBA interview preparation relies on peer-to-peer mock PIs, which are highly subjective, uncalibrated, and often blind to structural logic errors or subtle behavioral tells. Expensive coaching institutes can cost upward of ₹50,000, creating an unleveled playing field for CAT aspirants.

Our Solution

We trained our language models directly on thousands of successful and unsuccessful Indian B-School interview transcripts. NovaPrep's AI doesn't just listen—it builds a live decision tree of your answers, strictly evaluating you against the rigorous standards of top IIM panels in real time.
