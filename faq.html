FAQ

Common inquiries regarding our simulation technology, accuracy, and data protocols.

Where do the interview styles and transcripts come from?

NovaPrep’s simulations are generated in real-time using prompt-engineered language models tuned to common panel styles. We do not claim institutional endorsements or use proprietary university data. We produce simulated transcripts and structured feedback derived from those prompts.

Are you affiliated with IIM/FMS/XLRI?

No. We simulate panel styles used by those schools for practice purposes only. NovaPrep is not affiliated with any university.

How accurate is the system?

The system is a practice tool — it helps you rehearse structure, pacing, and handling pressure. It is not a substitute for live coaching but is designed to complement preparation.
